The Scoring Engine

Four axes.
One clear answer.

CircularIQ scores every valorisation pathway on four weighted axes — merging economic potential, technical feasibility, regulatory compliance, and market logistics into a single ranked recommendation.

Formula
Score = Eco×0.40 + Tech×0.30 + Reg×0.20 + Log×0.10
Any pathway with a blocked regulation receives score 0 and is removed from ranking — regardless of economic value.
