Common questions
Is the site assessment really free?
Yes — the initial site visit, stream measurement, and CircularIQ analysis are provided at no cost. You receive a full report within 2 weeks with no obligation to proceed.
What if my salinity is too high?
Streams with NaCl above 10 g/L trigger a hard gate that blocks biological pathways. We isolate those streams and scope Phase 3 evaporation-crystallisation options for salt recovery at €80–150/t.
How long does it take to see revenue?
The BioLink pilot unit is typically operational within 6 months. First certified algae batches can be listed on WasteMap at month 8. Full Year 2 net annual value is reached once all pathways stabilise.
What funding is available for the capex?
Several EU programmes apply: PRIMA (Mediterranean agrifood), LIFE (environment), Horizon Europe (green tech), and Italian PNRR funds. We help you identify and apply for the most suitable programme as part of the proposal.
What certifications do products carry?
Every certified output carries a 4-layer Circular Passport: regulatory compliance (EU 2020/741, FPR 2019/1009), process audit (LUM/CIHEAM or Bureau Veritas), circular economy claim (ICEA/Ecocert), and digital traceability (Circularise blockchain).
Can I buy without being a Puglia producer?
Absolutely. WasteMap is open to any verified buyer — farmers, aquaculture operators, energy users, municipalities — across Italy and the Mediterranean. Delivery logistics are coordinated by Giovanni P&F.
